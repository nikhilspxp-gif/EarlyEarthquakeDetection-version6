Using Machine Learning for Safer Communities in Developing Nations

Our research focuses on developing a low-cost, machine learning–based early earthquake detection system aimed at third-world countries.
The goal is to create an accessible and reliable method to warn communities before severe shaking begins, reducing injuries and damage.

Key Objectives

  • Integrate low-cost sensors with an edge ML model to detect seismic activity.
  • Utilize cloud-based data sharing for regional alert systems.
  • Develop a scalable and open-source framework for future research.
