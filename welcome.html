Welcome to Our Research Website

Thank you for visiting our project site! This platform summarizes our ongoing research on earthquake early detection using machine learning and affordable hardware.

Our Mission

Many developing countries lack the infrastructure for expensive seismic monitoring networks. We aim to bridge that gap with machine learning–based solutions that run on inexpensive sensors, enabling communities to receive alerts seconds before strong shaking begins.
